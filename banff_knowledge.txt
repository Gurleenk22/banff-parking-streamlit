Bear Street Parkade L1 reaches its highest average occupancy between 11 AM and 3 PM on weekends in July and August.
Central Park Lot has medium occupancy but rarely reaches full capacity.
Buffalo and Banff lots tend to fill quickly on sunny summer weekends between 10 AM and 2 PM.
Rainy days usually reduce overall occupancy across most lots.
Weekdays generally have lower occupancy than weekends, especially in early mornings and late evenings.
The classification model predicts whether a lot is near full based on weather, hour, day of week, capacity and recent occupancy.
The regression model predicts hourly occupancy counts, with temperature and month among the most important features.
